Children's Clinic

I love working with children as they are, in general, so receptive to things. As a mother of two children I understand what it means to want the best for your children and watching them suffer with illness is one of the most painful things in the world.

I am gentle and kind and I am fully aware that treating children is completely different to treating adults.

With obesity, diabetes, adhd and various other childhood illnesses and issues on the increase, nutrition plays a vital role. It is now well known to most that sugar plays a key part in the creation of a myriad of health problems and this can be exasperated in children.

My extensive knowledge and skill set allows me to support a variety of health problems such as sugar addictions, ADHD/ ADD, , allergies, anemia, anxiety, asthma, autism, celiac disease, chronic fatigue syndrome, cystitis, depression, failure to thrive and other eating disorders, eczema, food intolerances, obesity, psoriasis, reflux, skin problems, sleep problems, and tics and other involuntary movements.

I specialise in nutrition for children with frequent infections, behavioral problems, and digestive system disorders.

I realised that nutrition played a critical role in helping children and adults to overcome their complaints and illnesses. I believe that as parents and older children we can control the conditions by adjusting the food and drink that we consume, and the condition of our bodies.

Often with these symptoms clients have tried numerous allopathic methods, which may have initially supported them, but as a result have developed additional ailments, which are proving more difficult to treat. Or in many cases have had no success at all in treating the underlying cause of their ailment. With my knowledge I am able to look at the patient in their entirety, taking an in-depth case history from birth and allowing this to support her in her overall analysis and recommendations.

All treatments that I offer are physically painless and also allows the child to gain a sense of autonomy and satisfaction over their own health, which I believe in turn builds confidence.

  • Attention deficit disorder and ADHD
  • Allergies (food / and environmental)
  • Asthma
  • Baby Acne
  • Bed wetting
  • Common colds
  • Constipation
  • Colic
  • Dermatitis
  • Ear infections / Otitis Media
  • Eczema
  • Fever
  • Parasites
  • Seborrhea
  • Thrush
