About me

I originally studied Law with a view to becoming a Barrister. I had spent most of my teenage years and early twenties on various medications for lots of different illnesses. I got to a stage in my life when I thought, “this is ridiculous”, I had various side effects from all the medication and I decided that there had to be a better way.

I started looking at books and on the Internet for other ways to treat my symptoms. I finally decided that I needed to completely overhaul my life. I changed my diet and my life style radically. Within a few weeks I started to see an improvement. Within months I was off of all my medication, energetic and slimmer. I have been this way for nearly 15 years now and never looked back.

I was so interested in how, as human beings we have the capacity to heal ourselves and cure illnesses. I decided to train as a Nutritional Therapist. As I continued to train and my work as a therapist grew I was getting more and more fascinated by the body’s ability to heal itself from the inside. I then went on to train as a Naturopathic Doctor.

I have been practicing Nutrition and Naturopathic Medicine for 15 years now. I am a fully qualified Naturopathic Doctor and Nutritional Therapist. I am so passionate about my work and the wonders of the human body that I have dedicated my life to it. I am a single mother of two beautiful, healthy girls and whatever I practice in my clinic I practice at home.

I regularly fast, juice, and follow a raw food diet . I also frequently undertake vitamin flushes and liver/kidney cleanses. Any illness that my children or I have I treat with natural remedies.

I cover a wide variety of conditions and complaints but my main field of expertise is Gastro Enterology, (gut related complaints). I am very well respected in my field and have published articles. I think that the reason for this is because I have lived through illness and obesity and managed to completely change my life and I truly believe that you can do the same.

My practice is my passion and I have complete belief that every individual can take charge of their own body and feel great again. I will work with you, listen to you, and empower you to balance your diet, lose weight, prevent chronic disease, or eat right - whatever your goals may be. I can help you deal with the stress in your life and feel good again!

My primary focus is on a natural approach to health care. Clinical nutrition and lifestyle counselling are among the typical treatments that you could expect to receive, depending on your needs. "Naturopathic practitioners really believe in educating patients and engaging them in their own health care... I want you to feel empowered and take ownership over your health."
